our Services

With over 40 years of experience working on and for Fiat, we will keep your vehicle in top condition. We are a specialty shop with a narrow focus on engine and engine component repair and restoration on certain makes and models. We focus on Fiat (pre-1984) 850, X19, 128, 124, 2000, an Abarth models, and Lancia Beta and Scorpian models.

 

Most important of all: we love Fiats and Lancias and care about preserving and restoring yours.


We focus on the following engine component, however, we are available for full car care and repair on a case by case basis. 

- Transmissions
- Cylinder head modification / porting
- Caliper repair and rebuilding
- Perlux electronic ignition conversions to almost any distributor
